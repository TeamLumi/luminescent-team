Description

Works Key A large key for operating the doors of the Valley Windworks in the canyon. It was held by a Team Galactic Grunt. Floaroma Meadow

Super Rod An awesome, high-tech fishing rod. Use it at any body of water to fish for wild aquatic Pokémon. Snowpoint City
Galactic Key A card key for disengaging the security systems in the Galactic HQ. Losing it can result in punishment, apparently. Galactic Warehouse
Gracidea A flower sometimes bundled in bouquets to convey gratitude on special occasions, such as birthdays. Pokémon League
Oak's Letter A letter from Professor Oak. Written in it is a request for you to go to Route 224. Twinleaf Town
Oval Charm An oval charm said to increase the chance of Pokémon Eggs being found at the Nursery. Hotel Grand Lake
Shiny Charm A shiny charm said to increase the chance of finding a Shiny Pokémon in the wild. Hotel Grand Lake

Wind Badge The Wing badge, acquired from Heather. This item does absolutely nothing. Route 225
Fang Badge The Fang badge, acquired from Wyver. This badge is totally useless really. Route 226
Acid Badge The Acid badge, acquired from Slythe. This badge is just a pretty token. Route 227
Shadow Badge The Shadow badge, acquired from Shade. A reminder of the impending darkness, apparently. Route 228
General Badge The General badge, acquired from Gene. A token of victory over plain, but dangerous foes. Route 229
Moon Badge The Moon badge, acquired from Luna. A memento, as beautiful as the moon itself. Route 230

Rainbow Wing A mystical rainbow feather that sparkles. Eterna City
Azure Flute A flute that puts out echoing sounds that do not seem to be of this world. No one knows who made it. Hearthome City
