# Legendary Encounters
:::info
No Pokémon in Luminescent Platinum are shiny-locked. Knock out/run away from wild encounters to reset until shiny.
:::
<h3>Articuno</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0144_00_02_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Mt. Coronet (Summit)<br />
Level: 60<br />
<ul><li> Speak to Rowan after the Distortion World events.</li>
<li> Head to the snowy parts of Mt. Coronet.</li>
<li> Just above the spiralling paths entrance is a portal.</li>
<li> Talk to the portal to encounter Articuno.</li></ul><br /><br />

<h3>Mewtwo</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0150_01_00_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Oreburgh Gate<br />
Level: 70<br />
<ul><li> Once you gain access, meet with Dragonite in Resort Area to receive the Odd Invitation.</li>
<li> Go to Oreburgh Gate.</li>
<li> In the basement as far as in as you can go with Surf is a Nurse.</li>
<li> Talk to the Nurse with the Odd Invitation in your inventory to find Mewtwo.</li>
<li> Capture all of its clones to fight it.</li>
<li> After defeating its armored form, you will fight it again and may attempt to catch it.</li>
<li> Once defeated, you will receive [Mewtwo's Armor] to change its form back to Suppression Armor Mk 2.</li></ul>

<h3>Mew</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0151_00_02_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Route 201<br />
Level: 40<br />
<ul><li> A Shroomish is in the top<li>left of Route 203 near some trees.</li>
	<ul><li> Talk to it with an Oran Berry in your inventory. It'll take it, transform into Mew and disappear.</li></ul>
<li> After seeing the Shroomish part, you'll find a Buneary on Route 209 above a muddy slope.</li>
	<ul><li> Talk to it with a Sitrus Berry in your inventory. It'll take it, transform into Mew and disappear.</li></ul>
<li> After seeing the Buneary part, you'll find a Torchic in Pastoria City on the alcove above the lake.</li>
	<ul><li> Talk to it with an Old Gateau in your inventory. It'll take it, transform into Mew and disappear.</li></ul>
<li> After seeing the Torchic part, you'll find a Pachirisu on Route 201.</li>
	<ul><li> Talk to it and it'll transform into Mew, but stay in place.</ul>
<li> Talk to Mew to encounter it.</li></ul>

<h3>Arceus</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0493_00_02_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Hall of Origin<br />
Level: 80<br />
<ul><li> After the credits, talk to the old woman in the 'Foreign Building' in Hearthome City to make the Type Master trainers appear.</li>
<li> You need to collect all seventeen Plate items.</li>
<li> You can get the Plates via the Underground or by defeating the Type Master trainers. The Plates previously in the field have been removed.</li>
<li> Type Master trainers must be challenged with only a single Pokémon on your team that shares the type they specialise in.</li>
<li> Flame Plate: The Fire-type Type Master is found on Route 226, just right of the Survival Area.</li>
<li> Splash Plate: The Water-type Type Master is found on Route 219, just below Sandgem Town.</li>
<li> Zap Plate: The Electric-type Type Master is found in Sunyshore City, inside the lighthouse.</li>
<li> Meadow Plate: The Grass-type Type Master is found in Floaroma Meadow, just right of the house.</li>
<li> Icicle Plate: The Ice-type Type Master is found in Snowpoint City, to the right of the Gym.</li>
<li> Fist Plate: The Fighting-type Type Master is found in Veilstone City, in the house just south of the Gym.</li>
<li> Toxic Plate: The Poison-type Type Master is found in Pastoria City, on the top floor of the Great Marsh lobby.</li>
<li> Earth Plate: The Ground-type Type Master is found in Oreburgh Gate, on the top floor north of the entrances.</li>
<li> Sky Plate: The Flying-type Type Master is found at the Pokémon League, looking out on the right outside the building.</li>
<li> Mind Plate: The Psychic-type Type Master is found in Solaceon Ruins, just inside the entrance.</li>
<li> Insect Plate: The Bug-type Type Master is found on Route 202, just above the Sandgem Town entrance.</li>
<li> Stone Plate: The Rock-type Type Master is found on Route 207, just to the right of the muddy slope.</li>
<li> Spooky Plate: The Ghost-type Type Master is found in the Lost Tower, on the ground floor.</li>
<li> Draco Plate: The Dragon-type Type Master is found in Celestic Town, inside the ruins.</li>
<li> Dread Plate: The Dark-type Type Master is found in the Sendoff Spring, just after the entrance from Route 214.</li>
<li> Iron Plate: The Steel-type Type Master is found on Iron Island, just south of the entrance to the cave.</li>
<li> Pixie Plate: The Fairy-type Type Master is found in the Contest Hall.</li>
<li> With all seventeen Plates in your inventory, talk to the old woman in the 'Foreign Building' to get your second task: Complete the Pokédex.</li>
<li> Once your National Pokédex is completed to an extent that you're able to view the diploma in Valor Lakefront (i.e. you have obtained all Pokémon except for Mew, Ho-Oh, Lugia, Celebi, Jirachi, Deoxys, Phione, Manaphy, Darkrai, Shaymin, and Arceus), talk to the old woman again to receive the Azure Flute.</li>
<li> Go to Spear Pillar and stand on the flute symbol just as you leave the cave. You'll be prompted to play the Azure Flute.</li>
<li> Head up the stairs to battle Arceus.</li>
<li> You can fight Arceus repeatedly by playing the Azure Flute on the symbol until you catch it.</li></ul>
